Lasagna

What is lasagna?

Lasagna is a delicious dish. Why? Because it's lasagnicious. It takes a special chef to cook lasagna. Which? An awesome one. This recipe will use tomatoes and pasta and some spice...

Ingredients

  • Three tomatoes
  • Four lasagna pasta sheets
  • Water
  • Yummy meat (400 grams)
  • Spices (good ones)

Steps

  1. Mix the tomato into paste.
  2. Kiss the sheets to prepare them.
  3. Preheat the oven.
  4. Mince the meat and put it in the paste.
  5. Layer sheet - paste - spice
  6. Bake at 500 grees
  7. Eaty eat eat
